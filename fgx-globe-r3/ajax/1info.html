FGx Globe - r3

Flight data courtesy FGX Crossfeed

Things to do:

  • Open the Crossfeed panel. Click on a callsign. Opens a window dedicated to that flight.
  • Move your cursor over a plane. Opens the Heads-Up display with flight data.
  • Open the Permalink panel. Copy and bookmark the link to save your settings or to send them to someone else.
  • Left mouse / press 1 finger: Rotates
    Mousewheel / two fingers: Zooms
    Right mouse / press 2 fingers: Drags
