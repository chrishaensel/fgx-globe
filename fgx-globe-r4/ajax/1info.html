Flight data courtesy FGX Crossfeed

Things to do:

  • Click the Crossfeed panel. Click on a callsign. Opens a window dedicated to that flight
  • Move your cursor over a plane. Opens the Heads-Up display with flight data
  • Click the Permalink panel. Copy and bookmark the link to save your settings or to send the settings to someone
  • Click the Global Settings panel. Choose a different theme or select a different type of globe
General controls:
  • Rotate: Left mouse / press 1 finger
  • Zoom: Mousewheel / two fingers
  • Drag: Right mouse / press 2 fingers
Source Code and Read Me

System Requirements
  • Browser and GPU that supports WebGL
  • This app is under heavy development. Patience and forbearance are required.
  • This app is built and tested on the latest release of the Google Chrome browser. There are currently significant issues with FireFox, Safari and IE.
